## Improved Response

Reps (repetitions) and sets are basic concepts used in exercise training:

* **Repetition (Rep):** One complete movement of an exercise
  Example: Performing one full squat (down and back up) equals 1 rep

* **Set:** A group of repetitions performed consecutively before resting
  Example: 12 squats in a row = 1 set of 12 reps

If a workout says **3 sets of 12 reps**, you would:

1. Perform 12 repetitions
2. Rest
3. Repeat for a total of three sets

**Additional context:**

* 1–6 reps → strength
* 8–12 reps → muscle growth
* 12–20+ reps → endurance
